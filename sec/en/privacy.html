Privacy Policy

Respect for personal privacy and confidentiality is a consistent policy of KVB Kunlun. This "Privacy Policy" lists how KVB Kunlun will process your information when you log into this website.

KVB Kunlun is required to collect certain information to provide financial services and to meet your needs in customer service from time to time. The information required includes, but is not limited to, your name, date of birth, address, email address, telephone number, identification document, income and employment status. KVB Kunlun's Internet Service Provider may also record your information when you log in to this website, including but not limited to your functional variable name, login time, login content and browser category. If you choose not to provide the required information, KVB Kunlun may not be able to provide you with investment, trading or related services.

Your information can be used for day-to-day operations in order to provide services, as well as to process your account, review your ongoing needs, improve customer service and strengthen products development, promotion, transactions or related services and products for existing and potential customers, as well as giving ongoing information or opportunities related to you.

KVB Kunlun takes reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorized access or use by third parties. Depending on the operation and related products and services, KVB Kunlun may need to disclose your information to:

  1. any agent or third party service provider of KVB Kunlun;
  2. KVB Kunlun's affiliates or associates, which have undertaken to keep such information confidential;
  3. any person or institution that deals with you;
  4. an agent for credit reporting or reference;
  5. courts, tribunals and any statutory bodies or exchanges related to or supervised by KVB Kunlun, etc.

2. How do I withdraw funds from my account?

Customers can download the “Fund Withdrawal Form” on the file download page, complete it and return it to the customer service center. If the customer has established a standing order with us, then can apply by calling the customer service number.

3. How much money must be deposited in the margin account to trade?

The requirement for a margin account to trade is depended on the purchasing power in the account. If the client's account has sufficient purchasing power to purchase the stock, the purchasing power can be the cash value of the customer's account and/or the financing value of the stock position in the account.

4. How many times can the margin account be enlarged?

If you have opened a Margin account, you can buy and sell stocks in the form of margin. The deposit form for Hong Kong stocks can be found as “Margin Account Provision”on the download page.

5. How do I deposit physical stock?

You can hand over physical shares certificate to our Hong Kong office. If you place an order before 12:00 noon, the order will be processed on the same day, otherwise, the order will be processed until the second working day.

After depositing the physical stock, you need to wait until T+10 to sell the stock.

The name of the physical stock holder must be the same as the name of the stock account holder. If the physical stock is registered in the name of agent, you will need to provide proof of the ultimate beneficiary of the physical stock.

6. How do I withdraw physical stocks?

For stocks you bought, you can withdraw physical stocks after the settlement date. If you issue an order to withdraw the physical stock before 12:00 noon, it will be processed on the same day, otherwise, the order will be postponed until the second working day.

You can download the “Stock Withdrawal Form” from the download page, complete it and submit it to us. We generally need 3 working days to prepare physical stocks.

Customers are required to collect shares certificate in our Hong Kong office. When the physical stock is ready, our customer service staff will remind customer to pick up the physical stock by email or by phone.

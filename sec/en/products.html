The settlement of Hong Kong stocks after trading is carried out within the second working day after the trading day (ie T+2). However, the same stock can be bought and sold in the same day.

The Hong Kong stock market does not have the price limiting mechanism, and there is no limit up or limit down, except for SH-HK Connect and SZ-HK Connect.(SH-HK Connect and SZ-HK Connect price limiting mechanism: the previous closing price ±10%)

Initial Public Offer

IPO (Initial Public Offer) refers to the first time a company or institution issues new shares, bonds or certificates of deposit to public investors to raise funds. If you want to subscribe for IPO new shares, we can help you to arrange and handle all settlement procedures. You only need to open a securities account with KVB Kunlun Securities (HK) Limited to enjoy this service. The allocated shares will be deposited into your securities account on the allotment date so that you can sell the stocks on the first trading day.

About KVB

KVB Kunlun Securities (HK) Limited was established in 2007 and is a wholly-owned subsidiary of KVB Kunlun Holdings Limited. It is regulated by the Securities and Futures Commission of Hong Kong (SFC) and holds type 1, 2, 5 licenses, mainly for Securities, Futures and Options brokerage.

The parent company of KVB Kunlun Securities (HK) Limited - KVB Kunlun Holdings Limited has wholly-owned financial institutions in Auckland, Sydney, Melbourne, Toronto and Hong Kong, and regulated by authoritative regulators as well as authorized to provide various financial products. KVB Kunlun Securities (HK) Limited has established long-term cooperative relationships with many international well-known banks, exchanges and leading financial institutions in the business areas.

KVB Kunlun gathers financial experts together from different countries in the world to integrate multiculturalism, and committed to the development of international financial products and financial technology, breaking through the regional restrictions of traditional financial investment and services, meeting the demand of different regions, continuously improving service quality. KVB Kunlun won industrial and commercial trade service awards in many countries, and serves independent traders, fund managers, professional asset management institutions, financial institutions in the Asia-Pacific region, providing a wide range of trading products, as well as one-stop services and financial software systems.We adhere to the concept that create wealth with our customers and partners together.

Company Mission

KVB Kunlun Securities (HK) Limited adheres to the core mission of KVB Kunlun Group and is committed to providing customers with first-class customized financial services.

Through continuous exploration and innovation, KVB Kunlun Securities (HK) Limited enable customers to understand the operation mechanism of the securities market. And through the extensive exchange channels, KVB provides customers with rich products and professional services.

We firmly believe that the young and energetic KVB Kunlun Securities (HK) Limited can become another "Sparkling Star" of KVB Kunlun Group in Hong Kong, creating wealth and embracing opportunities with customers and partners in Greater China and even around the world.
